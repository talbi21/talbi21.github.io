ABOUT THE PROJECT

With Lost & found, you can always find what you lost or what you found. Featuring a filter that can reduce your search time, Lost & Found also incorporates a few charities that can support others once an item is lost forever. Also a security system that helps those who put a Found article, in fact, the latter will have the possibility of adding a secret question that only the real regulator of the object knows the answer, thus, we will have eliminated several messages occurring to this user
